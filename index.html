STRANGER THINGS

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror TV series set in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, during the 1980s. It follows a group of kids who uncover mysterious supernatural events after one of their friends disappears.

SEASON 1

Stranger Things Season 1 follows a group of friends searching for their missing friend, Will Byers. They meet Eleven, a mysterious girl with supernatural powers. Together, they uncover secret government experiments, a terrifying creature from the Upside Down, and fight to save Will while protecting their small town from danger.

SEASON 2

Stranger Things Season 2 takes place one year after Will Byers' rescue. Will struggles with disturbing visions from the Upside Down while a new creature, the Mind Flayer, threatens Hawkins. Eleven discovers more about her past, and the friends reunite to defeat the growing evil and protect their town.

SEASON 3

Stranger Things Season 3 is set during the summer of 1985. As friendships change and romance grows, a dangerous new threat emerges when the Mind Flayer returns. The friends battle terrifying monsters, uncover a secret Russian laboratory beneath Starcourt Mall, and risk everything to save Hawkins once again.

SEASON 4

Stranger Things Season 4 follows the friends as they face their greatest threat yet, Vecna, a powerful villain from the Upside Down. While separated across different locations, they uncover shocking secrets about Eleven's past. Together, they battle evil, protect Hawkins, and prepare for an even bigger final confrontation.

SEASON 5

Stranger Things Season 5 concludes the story as the friends reunite for one final battle against Vecna. Hawkins is under military quarantine, Eleven remains a target, and the Upside Down threatens the world. Together, they uncover long-hidden secrets, face heartbreaking sacrifices, and fight to save everyone they love.
